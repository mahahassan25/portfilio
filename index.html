About

Hello! I'm a passionate and driven software engineer specializing in front-end development with a strong foundation in backend technologies. My journey in technology began as a robotics instructor, where I had the privilege of teaching young minds the basics of programming with Scratch, electronics, and Logo. This experience not only strengthened my technical skills but also fostered my ability to break down complex concepts and communicate them effectively.

Currently, I work as a junior front-end developer, collaborating with a talented team where we primarily use Vue.js to create seamless and interactive web applications. I’ve also honed my skills in React, Nuxt.js, and MySQL, allowing me to deliver robust and efficient solutions tailored to modern web development needs. My ongoing study of Laravel is helping me transition into backend development, where I aim to build scalable and secure systems to complement my front-end expertise.

Beyond coding, I’m a lifelong learner who thrives on exploring new technologies and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in software development. I believe in crafting user-centric designs, writing clean and maintainable code, and continuously evolving to stay ahead in this ever-changing field.
